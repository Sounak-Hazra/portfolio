PROJECT OVERVIEW
----------------
A comprehensive solar energy prediction dataset with 1000 rows covering the years
2010-2024, designed for training machine learning models to predict solar energy
production based on environmental and meteorological features.

FEATURES BREAKDOWN
-----------------
Input Features (8):
  1. year              - Year from 2010 to 2024 (Integer)
  2. elevation         - Location elevation in meters (Float, 0-2500m)
  3. date              - Measurement date (String, YYYY-MM-DD)
  4. temperature       - Air temperature in Celsius (Float, -5°C to 35°C)
  5. humidity          - Relative humidity percentage (Float, 20-95%)
  6. solarIrradiance   - Solar irradiance in W/m² (Float, 50-1300 W/m²)
  7. cloudCover        - Cloud cover percentage (Float, 0-100%)
  8. windSpeed         - Wind speed in m/s (Float, 0-20 m/s)

Target Variable (1):
  9. solarEnergyGW     - Solar energy produced in Gigawatts (Float)

TECHNICAL DETAILS
----------------
Data Generation Method:
  • Realistic seasonal patterns (temperature, irradiance)
  • Geographic effects (elevation impact)
  • Weather relationships (cloud cover reduces irradiance)
  • Temporal trends (increasing solar capacity 2010-2024)
  • Random variation to simulate real-world uncertainty

Relationships Modeled:
  • Solar irradiance ~ season + cloud cover + elevation
  • Temperature ~ season + random variation
  • Humidity ~ inverse of temperature
  • Energy production ~ irradiance + year + efficiency
